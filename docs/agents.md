# AGENTS.md

# ReForge Agent Ecosystem

Each agent owns one responsibility.

## Repository Scout

Discovers repositories and collects metadata.

---

## Heritage Evaluator

Calculates the Heritage Score and Preservation Profile.

---

## Explorer

Analyzes the project structure.

---

## Historian

Reconstructs the project's history and purpose.

---

## Architect

Builds the architectural understanding of the software.

---

## Builder

Attempts to compile and execute the project.

---

## Restorer

Repairs build failures and restores functionality.

---

## Evolution Planner

Suggests future improvements while preserving identity.

---

## Validator

Verifies that every restoration is successful and documented.

---

## Supervisor

Coordinates all agents and manages workflow execution.

Agents communicate through structured outputs rather than natural language.
